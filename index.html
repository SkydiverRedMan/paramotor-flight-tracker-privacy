Privacy Policy - Paramotor Flight Tracker

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Paramotor Flight Tracker helps pilots record, review, import, and export flight information. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information the App Handles

  • Location and motion-related flight data, such as GPS coordinates, altitude, speed, heading, route, launch point, and timestamps.
  • Flight details that you enter, such as flight title, launch site, equipment, and notes.
  • Files that you choose to import or export, including flight logs and route files.
  • Purchase status for the optional Remove Ads in-app purchase.
  • Advertising and consent information handled by Google Mobile Ads and Google's User Messaging Platform.

How Information Is Used

Flight information is used to provide app features, including recording flights, showing return-to-launch guidance, saving a local flight history, reviewing routes, importing files, and exporting flight data.

Purchase status is used to determine whether ads should be hidden. Advertising and consent information is used to show ads when allowed and to manage privacy choices where required.

Storage and Sharing

Flight records are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them. Paramotor Flight Tracker does not operate its own server for collecting or storing your flight history.

If you export, share, back up, or import files using iOS sharing features or third-party apps, those files are handled according to the privacy practices of the services you choose.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK to display ads and Google's User Messaging Platform to manage consent messages. Google may process advertising-related information according to its own policies.

The app uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple processes purchase transactions according to Apple's privacy practices.

Location Permissions

The app requests location access so it can record flights and provide in-flight guidance. Background location access may be used to continue recording while the phone is locked or the app is not in the foreground.

Your Choices

  • You can manage location permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete saved flight files from within the app where deletion features are available.
  • You can use the optional Remove Ads purchase to hide banner ads.
  • Where required, you can manage ad privacy choices from the app's Settings screen.

Children's Privacy

Paramotor Flight Tracker is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
